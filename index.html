What we do.

Emergency response is just the beginning.

Fire Suppression

Structure fires, vehicle fires, wildland fires. We respond with the training and equipment to handle any situation.

Emergency Medical

First responder medical care. Our crews are trained EMTs ready to stabilize and assist until ambulance transport arrives.

Wildland Response

Montana means wildfire season. We maintain specialized crews and equipment for wildland urban interface protection.

Fire Prevention

Inspections, code enforcement, and community education. The best fire is the one that never starts.

Technical Rescue

Swift water rescue, ice rescue, vehicle extrication. Specialized teams for when standard response isn't enough.

Public Education

School visits, community events, CPR training. Building a fire-safe community starts with education.
